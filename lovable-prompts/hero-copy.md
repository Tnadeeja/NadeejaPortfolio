### Confidence Indicators:

- Quantifiable results where possible

### Humility Elements:
- "Currently developing" vs "Expert in"
- "Growing expertise in" vs "Master of"

- "Focused on learning" vs "Already know"

- Industry-standard terminology
- Clear, concise descriptions
- No slang or overly casual terms
- Appropriate technical depth
